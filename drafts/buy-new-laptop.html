Buses and sizes

SATA bus:
* SATA - (as HDD size) * mSATA (as mPCIe size). Width: 30 mm * Half size - 25 mm * Full size - 51 mm * M.2 SSD. Width: 22 mm * 30 mm * 42 mm * 60 mm * 80 mm

PCI-Express (PCIe) and NVM-Express (NVMe) buses: * mPCIe (as mSATA size). Width: 30 mm * M.2 SSD. Width: 22 mm * 30 mm * 42 mm * 60 mm * 80 mm * U.2

Notice: * NVMe needs to have a motherboard, that can boot it. Same goes for PCIe.
